## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
### Turn your food expertise into traffic — automatically.

Food Blog uses AI trained on food industry content to write professional blog posts that rank. Whether you run a restaurant, café, catering company, or food delivery brand, your blog is the most powerful SEO asset you're probably not using.

Stop paying for generic content writers who don't understand tasting menus, allergen compliance, or the difference between a brigade de cuisine and a meal prep service. Food Blog speaks your language.

**How it works:**
1. Choose your topic from 14 food-specific categories or write your own
2. Set your tone (professional, conversational, authoritative) and word count
3. AI generates a full SEO post with title, meta description, body, and tags
4. Review, edit, or publish directly to your Shopify blog in one click

**14 food-specific content categories:**
Nutrition · Gut Health · Skin Health · Allergens · Healthy Food Choices · Catering · Sport and Exercise · Mental Health · London (local) · Women's Health · Workplace Wellbeing · Sleep and Energy · Weight and Metabolism · Healthy Ageing

**Built for food businesses:**
- Set your business type (restaurant, bakery, café, deli, catering, meal prep, food delivery) — the AI adjusts accordingly
- Add your brand voice and dietary certifications (organic, halal, vegan, gluten-free) for consistent positioning
- Filter competitor mentions: Deliveroo, Uber Eats, Just Eat, and others can be excluded automatically
- Content calendar: pre-built topic suggestions for every month of the year

**SEO-ready output:**
Every post includes a keyword-optimised title, meta description, header structure, internal linking suggestions, and Shopify blog tags. No SEO experience required.

**Publish in one click:**
Connect your Shopify store and publish directly to your blog. Schedule posts, save as drafts, or auto-publish on generation. Track all content from a single dashboard.

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. Food-specific AI that actually knows your industry**
Unlike generic AI writers, Food Blog understands allergen legislation, dietary certifications, catering terminology, and food trend cycles. Every post sounds like it was written by someone who works in food — because the AI was trained that way.

**2. Go from zero to published in under 2 minutes**
Choose a topic, hit generate, review, publish. No briefs, no back-and-forth, no waiting for a copywriter to deliver. Your team can publish 3–5 posts per week without any writing skill.

**3. SEO traffic that compounds over time**
Blog content is your highest-ROI marketing channel. A post written today can drive search traffic for years. Food Blog helps you build that library fast, with titles and descriptions that match what your customers are actually searching for.

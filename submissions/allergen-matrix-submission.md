## 4. Full Description

### The world's allergen laws in one Shopify app.

Whether you sell in the UK, the US, the EU, or Australia — allergen labelling is the law. Getting it wrong causes real harm and carries serious legal consequences. Allergen Matrix gives food retailers, delis, caterers, and restaurants a simple, compliant way to manage and display allergen information across every jurisdiction.

**Select your compliance mode:**
- 🇬🇧 **UK — Natasha's Law** (UK Food Information Regulations 2014): 14 allergens. Full pre-packed food labelling required.
- 🇺🇸 **US — FALCPA + FASTER Act**: 9 major allergens including sesame (added 2023). Tree nuts must be individually named. New York requires sesame labelling from November 12, 2025.
- 🇪🇺 **EU — FIR 1169/2011**: 14 allergens. Mandatory on all pre-packed food sold in EU member states.
- 🇦🇺 **AU — FSANZ Standard 1.2.3**: 11 allergens. Required on all food sold in Australia and New Zealand.
- 🌍 **Global**: Show all allergens from all regimes — ideal for international stores.

**How it works:**
1. Select your compliance mode in the admin — the correct allergen set loads automatically.
2. Tag each product with its allergens and dietary suitability.
3. Your storefront displays a live, filterable allergen matrix with a compliance badge.
4. Customers filter by allergen to find safe products — exclusion-based (hides products containing the allergen, not just shows allergen-free ones).

**What's built — no false claims:**
- Live allergen matrix with desktop table + mobile card views
- Compliance mode selector with automatic allergen set (UK/US/EU/AU/Global)
- US-specific tree nut specification (FALCPA requires naming specific tree nuts)
- New York 2025 sesame note displayed automatically in US mode
- Compliance badge on storefront ("✓ Natasha's Law Compliant", "✓ FALCPA Compliant", etc.)
- Dietary filters: Vegan, Vegetarian, Halal, Gluten-free, Nut-free, Dairy-free
- Exclusion-based filtering — the correct allergy safety model
- Bulk add to basket from filtered results
- Custom brand colour (Pro)
- Bulk CSV import from existing records (Starter+)
- Audit log — every allergen change timestamped (Pro)

**What's NOT in this app:**
- No QR code generator (listed as future roadmap, not built)
- No compliance report export (roadmap)
- No staff training mode (roadmap)

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. One app for every major allergen jurisdiction**
Switch between UK (Natasha's Law), US (FALCPA + FASTER Act), EU (FIR 1169/2011), and AU (FSANZ) with a single dropdown. The correct allergen set, compliance badge, and legal citation update automatically. No configuration required.

**2. Protect your customers — and your business**
Incorrect allergen information causes real harm. Allergen Matrix uses exclusion-based filtering: customers mark what they must avoid, and the matrix hides everything that contains it. This is the correct model for serious allergy management.

**3. US businesses: FALCPA-compliant tree nut labelling built in**
US law requires specific tree nuts to be named individually (not just "tree nuts"). Allergen Matrix prompts you to declare which nuts your products contain, and surfaces the New York 2025 sesame note automatically. Correct from day one.

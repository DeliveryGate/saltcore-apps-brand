## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
### Food allergen labelling isn't optional ‚Äî make it effortless.

In the UK and EU, incorrect allergen labelling can result in prosecutions, civil claims, and devastating harm to customers. Allergen Matrix gives food retailers, delis, caterers, and restaurants a simple, compliant way to manage and display allergen information ‚Äî without spreadsheets, manual updates, or expensive compliance software.

**The 14 EU allergens, fully managed:**
Celery ¬∑ Crustaceans ¬∑ Eggs ¬∑ Fish ¬∑ Gluten ¬∑ Lupin ¬∑ Milk ¬∑ Molluscs ¬∑ Mustard ¬∑ Peanuts ¬∑ Sesame ¬∑ Soya ¬∑ Sulphites ¬∑ Tree nuts

**Dietary modes included:**
Nut-free ¬∑ Gluten-free ¬∑ Halal ¬∑ Vegetarian ¬∑ Vegan

**How it works:**
1. Tag each product with its allergens and dietary suitability
2. Allergen Matrix syncs to your storefront as a live, filterable matrix
3. Customers filter by allergen to find safe meals ‚Äî exclusion-based (hiding meals that contain an allergen, not just showing allergen-free)
4. Your data stays up to date as your menu changes

**Exclusion-based filtering ‚Äî the right way:**
Most allergen tools show "allergen-free" products. We flip this: customers mark what they're allergic to, and the matrix hides everything that contains it. This is the correct model for serious allergy management and matches how allergy professionals think about food safety.

**For your operations team:**
- Bulk CSV import from your existing records ‚Äî import everything in minutes
- CSV export for kitchen teams, health inspectors, or insurance documents
- Product completeness scoring: see which products still need allergen data
- Staff training mode (Pro): walk your team through the matrix during onboarding
- Audit log (Pro): every allergen change timestamped with staff member ‚Äî for regulatory compliance

**For your storefront:**
The Allergen Matrix Shopify theme block installs with one click. It displays as a desktop table and mobile card view. Customers can filter and find safe items, add selected items to their basket, and load more without page refresh.

**For regulatory requirements:**
- Follows Natasha's Law (UK Food Information Amendment 2021)
- Follows EU FIC Regulation No 1169/2011
- US FALCPA format available on Pro
- Compliance report export for food safety audits

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. Comply with UK and EU allergen labelling law ‚Äî without a spreadsheet**
Natasha's Law requires clear allergen labelling on every pre-packed food product. Allergen Matrix gives you a single source of truth: manage allergens in the admin, display them on your storefront, and export compliance reports for inspectors ‚Äî all from one place.

**2. Protect your customers and your business**
Incorrect allergen information causes real harm. Allergen Matrix's exclusion-based filtering ensures customers only see meals that are safe for them, reducing the risk of an accidental exposure and the legal liability that comes with it.

**3. Turn compliance into a customer experience**
Your allergen matrix is visible, filterable, and beautiful. Customers with allergies or dietary requirements don't have to ring ahead, check labels, or trust a server's memory ‚Äî they can self-serve directly on your website. That reduces friction and builds lasting trust.

## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Selling alcohol, tobacco, CBD, adult content, or any age-restricted product on Shopify? You need an age gate — and it needs to actually work.

AgeGuard adds a professional, full-screen age verification overlay to your Shopify store the moment a visitor arrives. They verify their age once, and a cookie remembers their verification for as long as you choose. Return visitors go straight to your store. No friction, no repeated interruptions.

**Why AgeGuard instead of a free theme trick?**
Free age gates are typically a single "Are you 18?" button — easy to click past, with no date of birth check, no compliance logging, no customisation, and no geo-targeting. AgeGuard is built properly.

**Three verification modes:**

**Simple** — A "I confirm I am over 18" button with your brand messaging. Fast, minimal friction. Suitable where a declaration is legally sufficient.

**Age Input** — Customer enters their age (year only or full date of birth). AgeGuard validates the age before granting access. Blocks anyone entering an age below your threshold.

**Date of Birth** — Full date of birth entry (day, month, year). The strictest verification mode. Required in some jurisdictions for alcohol and adult products.

**How it works:**
1. Install AgeGuard and choose your verification mode from the admin
2. Customise the overlay: background image or colour, logo, heading text, button text, colour scheme
3. Set your cookie duration (e.g. 30 days — verified visitors won't see the gate again for 30 days)
4. Optionally exempt specific pages (your About or Contact page) from the age gate
5. The gate appears for every new visitor, with verification required before they can browse
6. Verified visitors set to bypass — logged-in customer accounts can be automatically exempted

**Compliance features (Pro):**
- Geolocation rules — show different verification requirements based on country
- Compliance logging — record verification attempts for audit purposes
- Export verification log to CSV

**One of the most-searched apps for alcohol, CBD, and adult product stores on Shopify.**

### Key Benefits

**1. A Real Age Gate, Not a "Click to Confirm" Button**
Most free age gate solutions are a single yes/no button anyone can click through. AgeGuard's Date of Birth mode requires a genuine date entry that is validated before the visitor can proceed — meaningful age verification, not a legal fig leaf.

**2. Remember Verified Visitors — No Repeated Friction**
Once a visitor verifies, a cookie remembers them for however long you set (1 day to 365 days). Return visitors go straight to your store. Verified logged-in customers are automatically exempted. Your age gate protects without annoying your real customers.

**3. Fully Customisable to Match Your Brand**
A clunky, generic age gate damages your brand. AgeGuard's overlay is fully customisable — your logo, your colours, your messaging, your background image. The first thing a visitor sees in your store looks like it belongs there.

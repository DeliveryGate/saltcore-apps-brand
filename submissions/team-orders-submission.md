## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
### Sell weekly office lunches through your Shopify store — without a bespoke ordering system.

If you cater for offices, co-working spaces, film sets, or corporate clients, you know the pain: orders arrive by email or WhatsApp, portions change at the last minute, and your kitchen never has a clean run sheet for Monday morning. Team Orders brings weekly B2B catering onto your Shopify store with a structured, repeatable ordering flow that works the same way every week.

**How it works:**
1. Configure your meal sizes, prices, available days, and minimum order quantities in the admin
2. The Team Orders block appears on any Shopify page — your clients use it to build their weekly order
3. Clients pick days, set quantities, enter their team and allergy notes, and select their week commencing date
4. The full weekly order is added to the Shopify cart as separate line items per day — your normal fulfilment flow handles the rest

**What clients experience:**
- Day-by-day toggle: select which days the office is ordering (Mon–Fri)
- Meal size selector with live pricing per day
- Quantity input per active day
- Live weekly total calculation that updates as they adjust
- Postcode check widget (you control which postcodes you deliver to)
- Team notes and allergy notes field
- Week commencing date picker
- Single "Add week to cart" — all days added as separate line items

**What you configure in the admin:**
- Available days of the week
- Popular days highlighting (e.g. "Most popular" badge on Wednesday)
- Meal sizes with base price and per-portion price
- Minimum order quantity per day
- Advance notice requirement (e.g. orders must be placed by Wednesday for the following week)
- Volume discount tiers with custom messaging
- Allergen disclaimer text
- Postcode delivery zone
- Page title, intro text, and hero customisation

**Recurring orders (Pro+):**
Regular corporate clients can save their weekly order profile and reorder in seconds. The system remembers their days, quantities, notes, and delivery postcode — reducing friction for your best customers.

**Connects to Corporate Accounts:**
Team Orders integrates with the Corporate Accounts app. Approved corporate clients get their account pricing applied automatically, and their orders are tracked against their monthly invoice (requires Corporate Accounts Pro plan).

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. Structured weekly ordering reduces kitchen errors and last-minute changes**
When orders arrive by email or WhatsApp, they're inconsistent, late, and ambiguous. Team Orders gives clients a structured form: days, quantities, notes, week commencing date. Your kitchen gets a clean run sheet every time, well in advance.

**2. Volume discounts close larger office accounts**
Set tiered volume discounts — 10% off 10+ portions, 15% off 20+ — and display them prominently in the order flow. Corporate clients ordering 5 days a week are price-sensitive. A clearly displayed discount at exactly the right moment converts hesitant prospects into loyal accounts.

**3. Minimum order enforcement protects your margins**
Small orders for one or two days can be uneconomical when you factor in delivery. Team Orders lets you set a minimum order quantity per day and a minimum weekly total. Clients see their running total and can't submit until they've met the threshold — without any awkward conversations.

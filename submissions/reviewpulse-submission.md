## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
93% of Shopify stores have no product reviews. Not because their customers are unhappy — because they never ask. ReviewPulse fixes that by automatically requesting reviews from customers after every order, then making it simple to collect, manage, and display them on your store.

Built for merchants who understand that social proof sells — but don't want to spend hours chasing reviews or managing a complex review platform.

**How ReviewPulse works:**

*Collecting reviews:*
Automatically send a review request email to customers a set number of days after their order is fulfilled (you choose when — typically 7–14 days). Customers click through to a simple review form, rate the product out of 5 stars, and leave a comment. No account required.

*Displaying reviews:*
The ReviewPulse theme extension adds a star rating widget to your product pages, showing the average rating, review count, and individual reviews. A star badge shows ratings on collection pages so customers can see top-rated products at a glance.

*Managing reviews:*
Every submitted review comes into your ReviewPulse admin as "pending approval." Read the review, approve or decline it, and approved reviews go live on your product page instantly. You're always in control of what's displayed.

**What's included:**
- Star rating widget on product pages (theme extension — no code required)
- Review submission form (theme extension)
- Star badge on collection pages
- Automated post-purchase review request emails
- Admin dashboard — approve, reject, flag, reply to reviews
- Photo reviews (Starter and Pro) — customers can attach images
- Review request campaigns — email all past customers who haven't reviewed
- Import reviews from other platforms (Pro)
- Analytics — average rating, review volume, response rate

**Reviews are the single most persuasive element on a product page.** ReviewPulse gets you them automatically.

---

### Key Benefits

**1. Automate Your Review Collection Completely**
Set it up once — ReviewPulse emails every customer automatically after their order arrives. You don't need to remember to ask, chase anyone, or manually send emails. Reviews come in on autopilot.

**2. Full Control — You Approve Every Review**
No review goes live without your approval. ReviewPulse holds all submissions in a moderation queue. You read, approve, and publish the reviews you're happy with. You can also reply to reviews publicly, showing customers you listen.

**3. Show Star Ratings Everywhere — Product Pages and Collection Pages**
The average star rating appears on each product page as a full review widget, and as a compact star badge on collection pages. Customers can filter and sort by rating. Social proof where it matters most — where buying decisions are made.

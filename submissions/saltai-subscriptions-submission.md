## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)

SaltAI Subscriptions gives food businesses the infrastructure to sell recurring orders on Shopify — without Shopify Plus or complex third-party middleware.

Whether you're running a weekly meal box scheme, a monthly hamper subscription, or a recurring corporate lunch order, SaltAI Subscriptions handles the full lifecycle: sign-up, payment, fulfilment scheduling, and customer self-management.

**Built for food businesses, not SaaS companies.** Most subscription apps are built around digital products or physical retail. SaltAI Subscriptions is designed around the realities of food commerce: variable order windows, dietary preferences, allergen data, skip weeks, pause requests, and delivery schedule management.

**How it works:**
1. Mark any product as subscribable and set your billing intervals (weekly, fortnightly, monthly)
2. Customers subscribe at checkout with their chosen frequency
3. Orders are automatically created and charged on schedule
4. Customers manage their subscription via a self-service portal

**What's included:**
- Recurring billing via Shopify Billing API (no external payment processor needed)
- Customer subscription portal — skip, pause, cancel, update delivery address
- Flexible billing intervals: weekly, fortnightly, monthly, custom
- Subscription discount support (e.g. 10% off for subscribers)
- Admin dashboard: active subscriptions, upcoming charges, churn tracking
- Email notifications for upcoming charges, successful payments, and failures
- Dunning management: automatic retry on failed payments
- Allergen and dietary preference capture at subscription sign-up

## 5. Key Benefits (ready to paste)

**1. Predictable Revenue for Your Food Business**
Subscriptions transform one-off orders into guaranteed recurring income. Know exactly how much you'll produce and sell each week, reduce food waste, and build a loyal customer base who pays before the order goes out.

**2. No Shopify Plus Required**
Built on the Shopify Billing API and available to all Shopify plan tiers. Get subscription functionality that would normally require a £2,000+/month platform upgrade, at a fraction of the cost.

**3. Customer Self-Service That Reduces Admin**
The built-in subscriber portal lets customers skip weeks, pause deliveries, update their address, and manage payment — without emailing you. Dramatically reduces subscription-related support queries.

## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Your checkout is where orders are won or lost. Delivery IQ gives you complete control over the delivery experience — so customers only order what you can actually fulfil, and bulk buyers get rewarded automatically.

Built for food merchants, florists, artisan producers, and any Shopify store with real-world delivery constraints.

**The problem Delivery IQ solves:**
- Customers order for delivery dates you can't fulfil (bank holidays, closed days, sold-out slots)
- You're losing orders because you can't offer delivery to certain postcodes
- You want to reward bulk buyers with automatic discounts but don't want to manage discount codes
- Your checkout has no way to collect delivery instructions or special requests

**How it works:**
1. Set your delivery zones by postcode prefix (AB, EC1, SW3, etc.)
2. Block specific dates you can't deliver (bank holidays, holidays, events)
3. Set volume discount thresholds — e.g. 10% off orders of 5+ items, 15% off 10+
4. Customers see real-time delivery eligibility and discounts at checkout
5. Orders with invalid postcodes or blocked dates are caught before payment

**Features:**
- Postcode prefix validation — only accept orders from your delivery area
- Blocked date management — block individual dates or recurring days of the week
- Time slot display — show available delivery windows to customers
- Minimum order value enforcement by delivery zone
- Volume discount function — automatic discounts applied at order level
- Delivery fee tiers based on order value
- Thank You page delivery summary — shows customers their confirmed delivery details
- All settings managed from the admin dashboard — no developer needed

**Real-world example:** A London meal prep business uses Delivery IQ to only accept orders from N, E, SE, SW, and W postcodes. They block Mondays (prep day) and bank holidays. Orders of 5+ meal packs get 10% off automatically. The result: zero cancelled orders, higher average basket size.

---

### Key Benefits

**1. Zero Undeliverable Orders**
Stop accepting orders you can't fulfil. Set your delivery postcodes and blocked dates once — Delivery IQ catches any incompatible orders before the customer pays. No more refund requests, no more disappointed customers.

**2. Automatic Volume Discounts — No Discount Codes Required**
Incentivise larger orders without creating, managing, or sharing discount codes. Set your thresholds (e.g. 5+ items = 10% off) and Delivery IQ's Shopify Function applies discounts automatically at checkout.

**3. Full Delivery Control From One Dashboard**
Manage your entire delivery operation from a single admin screen. Update postcodes, block new dates, adjust time slots, and change minimum order values instantly — no developer, no code changes.

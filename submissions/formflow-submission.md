## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Every Shopify store eventually needs a form — a contact form, an event registration, a wholesale enquiry, a feedback survey, a competition entry. But Shopify's built-in contact form is the only one you get, and it's a single, fixed form with no customisation.

FormFlow gives you a full form builder for Shopify. Create any form you can imagine, embed it anywhere in your store, collect submissions in your dashboard, and get email notifications when someone submits.

**What you can build with FormFlow:**
- Contact and support forms
- Wholesale and trade account enquiry forms
- Event registration and booking request forms
- Competition entry and giveaway forms
- Feedback and satisfaction survey forms
- Product customisation request forms
- Job application forms
- Newsletter signup forms with custom fields

**Field types available:**
Text, Textarea, Email, Phone, Number, Dropdown (Select), Checkboxes, Radio buttons, Date picker, File upload, Hidden field

**How it works:**
1. Open the FormFlow builder in your Shopify admin
2. Drag fields onto the form canvas, set labels, placeholders, and validation
3. Mark required fields, set character limits, add help text
4. Copy the embed code and paste it into any Shopify page, blog post, or section
5. Submissions arrive in your FormFlow dashboard and trigger email notifications
6. Export all submissions to CSV for reporting or follow-up

**Security built in:**
Every FormFlow form includes honeypot spam protection — a hidden field that catches bots without annoying CAPTCHAs. File upload fields include size and type restrictions to prevent abuse.

**For merchants who need more than a contact form — without paying for a full CRM.**

### Key Benefits

**1. Any Form, Anywhere in Your Store**
Contact page, product page, homepage, blog post — FormFlow forms embed anywhere with a simple shortcode. Build forms for any purpose without touching your theme code.

**2. All Submissions in One Place**
Stop hunting for form submissions in your email inbox. Every response is stored in your FormFlow dashboard, searchable, filterable, and exportable to CSV. Follow up with confidence, export for your CRM, or share with your team.

**3. Built-in Spam Protection — No CAPTCHA Friction**
Honeypot spam filtering catches bots automatically without showing customers an annoying CAPTCHA. Your forms stay clean, your inbox stays manageable, and your customers don't get frustrated.

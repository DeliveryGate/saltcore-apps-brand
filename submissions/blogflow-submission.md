## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Every Shopify merchant knows blogging drives traffic — but who has the time? BlogFlow uses AI to write high-quality, SEO-optimised blog posts tailored to your products and store, and publishes them directly to your Shopify blog.

Not another generic AI content tool. BlogFlow generates content that's relevant to your actual products, your audience, and your industry — with proper SEO structure baked in from the start.

**What BlogFlow creates for you:**
- Long-form blog posts (600–1500 words) written around your target keywords
- Proper H1/H2/H3 heading structure for SEO
- Meta titles and descriptions optimised for click-through
- Internal links to your products automatically included
- Readable, engaging prose — not keyword-stuffed filler

**How it works:**
1. Enter your topic or target keyword in the admin dashboard
2. Choose your tone (professional, friendly, authoritative) and word count
3. BlogFlow generates a complete, SEO-structured post in under 30 seconds
4. Review in the built-in editor, make any edits you want
5. Publish immediately to your Shopify blog, or schedule for later
6. Track published posts and see which drive traffic

**Who it's for:**
- Food businesses who want to rank for local and recipe searches
- Shopify merchants who know they need blog content but don't have time to write
- Stores using Shopify's built-in blog but not updating it regularly
- Merchants investing in SEO who want to scale their content output

**Built-in SEO features:**
- Keyword density optimisation
- Suggested internal links to your product pages and existing posts

BlogFlow connects directly to your Shopify blog via the Admin API — posts appear as native Shopify blog articles, fully editable, with no third-party hosting or redirects.

---

### Key Benefits

**1. Rank on Google Without Hiring a Copywriter**
Each BlogFlow post is built around your target keyword with proper heading structure, natural keyword placement, and meta data that search engines understand. Get professional SEO content at a fraction of the cost.

**2. From Idea to Published in Under 2 Minutes**
Enter a topic, review the draft, click publish. BlogFlow handles everything else — SEO structure, word count, tone, internal links to your products. No brief to write, no back-and-forth with a writer.

**3. Your Blog, Fully Under Your Control**
Posts publish directly to your native Shopify blog — not an iframe, not an external blog. Full editorial control, editable in Shopify admin, with your store's SEO domain authority.

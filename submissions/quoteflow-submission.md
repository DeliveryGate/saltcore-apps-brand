## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Wholesale and B2B selling on Shopify often means fielding pricing enquiries by email, WhatsApp, or phone — with no record, no system, and no way to scale. QuoteFlow brings your B2B quoting process into Shopify where it belongs.

Add a professional "Request a Quote" button to any product page. Customers submit their requirements — quantity, delivery requirements, special requests. You review in the QuoteFlow admin, respond with a custom price, and the customer gets a secure link to pay. No back-and-forth over email. No lost quotes.

**Who QuoteFlow is for:**
- Food wholesalers and catering suppliers who sell different prices to different customers
- Shopify merchants with minimum order quantities that vary by customer
- B2B stores where pricing depends on volume, contract, or relationship
- Any merchant who receives pricing enquiries and wants to handle them professionally

**The QuoteFlow workflow:**
1. Customer clicks "Request a Quote" on any product page
2. They fill in their requirements (quantity, delivery date, notes, company name)
3. You receive the quote request in your Shopify admin (and by email notification)
4. Review, adjust the price, and send a quote back to the customer
5. Customer gets an email with a secure checkout link at the agreed price
6. They pay through your normal Shopify checkout — order appears as normal

**Features:**
- "Request a Quote" button on product pages (theme extension, no code required)
- Quote submission form with custom fields
- Admin dashboard — view all quotes, filter by status (pending, quoted, accepted, declined)
- Quote response editor — set your price, add notes, set expiry
- Secure checkout link generation at the quoted price
- Email notifications for new quotes (merchant) and responses (customer)
- Rate limiting — prevents spam quote submissions
- Quote history per customer

**Different customers, different prices — handled professionally.**

---

### Key Benefits

**1. Stop Losing B2B Enquiries to Email Chaos**
Every quote request that comes in by email is a quote you might lose track of. QuoteFlow centralises all quote requests in your Shopify admin — with status tracking, customer details, and a full audit trail. Nothing gets missed.

**2. Set Custom Prices Per Customer**
B2B pricing isn't one-size-fits-all. With QuoteFlow, you set the price for each quote individually. The customer pays exactly what you agreed — through your normal Shopify checkout, with your normal fulfilment flow.

**3. Professional B2B Experience Without a Custom Build**
Your B2B customers expect a professional buying experience. A "Request a Quote" button, a proper response with pricing, and a secure checkout link — QuoteFlow delivers all of that without any custom development.

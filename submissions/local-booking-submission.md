## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
### Keep bookings on your own store — not a third-party platform that charges you per booking.

Local Booking gives independent service businesses — laundries, dry cleaners, storage facilities, luggage services, tailors, and local specialists — a fully configurable slot booking system built directly into Shopify. Your customers book on your site, you manage availability in your admin, and you own the relationship.

**Designed for drop-off and collection services:**
Local Booking is built specifically for businesses that accept drop-offs and schedule collections — the kind of booking journey that most generic appointment apps don't handle well. Customers choose a drop-off slot, you process the service, they book a collection slot.

**How it works:**
1. Set up your service types and pricing in the admin
2. Configure your time slots, operating hours, and capacity per slot
3. Install the Local Booking theme block on any Shopify page
4. Customers select their service, choose a slot, and complete checkout on Shopify

**What customers experience:**
- Service selection with pricing per day
- Drop-off and collection slot picker
- Overnight service support (for services with multi-day turnaround)
- Optional add-on services (e.g. express, alteration, repair)
- Location details with map link and Waze navigation
- Trust messages and pickup instructions at checkout

**What you manage in the admin:**
- Service types with per-day pricing
- Operating hours per day of week (Starter+)
- Time slot configuration with capacity limits per slot (Pro+)
- Add-on services with online/offline payment tracking (Starter+)
- Booking status management (pending, confirmed, ready, collected)
- Monthly booking count tracking and limit enforcement
- Calendar view of all bookings (Pro+)

**Built for physical, local service businesses:**
Local Booking isn't a generic appointment app. It includes features specific to physical service businesses: overnight handling, capacity per slot (so you don't overbook a van), Waze/maps integration for directions, and trust messages that address the most common customer hesitations ("Will my items be safe?").

**No monthly platform fee from a third party:**
Calendly, Booksy, and similar tools charge per booking or per month — and they own your customer relationships. Local Booking runs inside your Shopify store. You pay once, and you keep all the data.

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. Accept bookings on your own website, not a third-party platform**
Every booking made through a third-party platform is a customer relationship you don't fully own. Local Booking keeps bookings inside your Shopify store — your customer data, your re-marketing list, your brand experience from start to finish.

**2. Manage drop-off and collection separately with capacity per slot**
Most booking apps are built for single appointments. Local Booking handles the two-trip pattern — drop-off and collection — as first-class features. Set capacity per slot so you never accept more drop-offs than your team can handle in a morning.

**3. Overnight and multi-day services, handled correctly**
If your turnaround takes 24–72 hours, your booking system needs to reflect that. Local Booking supports overnight services natively — customers select drop-off, see available collection slots based on your processing time, and book both in one flow.

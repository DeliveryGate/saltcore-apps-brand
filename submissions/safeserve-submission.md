## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
Running a food business on Shopify means more than just great products — it means keeping your customers informed and your store compliant.

SafeServe makes allergen labelling simple. Add clear, professional allergen badges to any product page in minutes, with no code required. Choose from 14 major allergens (including all UK Natasha's Law requirements), dietary certifications like Halal, Vegan, and Gluten Free, and full nutrition labels in UK or FDA format.

**Built for Shopify food merchants who need to:**
- Comply with UK Natasha's Law and EU FIC Regulation 1169/2011
- Display allergen information clearly and honestly
- Build trust with customers who have dietary requirements
- Manage allergen data across their product catalogue efficiently

**How it works:**
1. Install SafeServe and configure your allergen data from the admin dashboard
2. The theme extension adds professional badges to your product pages automatically
3. Customers see clear allergen icons, dietary labels, and nutrition information
4. Update allergen data in one place — changes reflect across your store instantly

**What's included:**
- 14 major allergen badges (celery, cereals containing gluten, crustaceans, eggs, fish, lupin, milk, molluscs, mustard, nuts, peanuts, sesame, soya, sulphites)
- Dietary certification badges (Halal, Kosher, Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Dairy Free, Organic)
- Full nutrition label display (UK GDA format and US FDA format)
- Dietary filter bar for collection pages
- Bulk import via CSV for large product catalogues
- Mobile-responsive design that matches your theme

**Trusted by food businesses, built by food merchants.** SafeServe was developed from our own experience running Vanda's Kitchen — a halal catering business on Shopify. We know exactly what food merchants need.

## 4. Full Description (ready to paste)
### Your collection page should find the right product for every customer — automatically.

Food and wellness shoppers know exactly what they can and can't eat. Gluten intolerance, vegan lifestyle, halal dietary requirements, nut allergies — your collection page needs to reflect this reality, or customers leave. Smart Shop gives your Shopify collection intelligent dietary filtering that works the way food-conscious customers think.

**How it works:**
1. Tag your products with dietary properties and categories in the Smart Shop admin
2. Configure your filter tiles and dietary options
3. Smart Shop adds a filterable collection block to your storefront
4. Customers click what they need — Smart Shop hides products that don't match

**Exclusion-based filtering — the correct approach:**
Smart Shop uses exclusion logic. Customers select what they're avoiding (e.g. gluten, dairy, meat), and the collection hides everything that contains it. This is safer and more intuitive than "contains" filters — it mirrors how allergy-conscious shoppers actually think.

**What your customers see:**
- Square emoji filter tiles with dietary labels (not boring dropdown menus)
- Product cards with allergen chips showing what's in each item
- A morphing quantity stepper: products show "Add to cart" on first tap, then "– 1 +" for adjustments — no page reload
- Volume discount banner across the collection
- Product search bar within the filtered view
- Sidebar layout on desktop, horizontal scroll tiles on mobile

**What you manage in the admin:**
- Product categories (set your own: Main, Side, Dessert, Drink, etc.)
- Dietary tags per product (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, halal, dairy-free, nut-free)
- Portion sizes and serving info
- Volume discount tiers (e.g. 10% off 5+ items, 20% off 10+)
- Promotional banner with custom text, link, and accent colour
- Analytics on filter usage patterns (Pro+)

**Works with your existing products:**
Smart Shop reads your existing Shopify product catalogue. You enrich products with dietary and category data in the Smart Shop admin — your product listings in Shopify stay unchanged.

## 3 Key Benefits

**1. Stop losing dietary-conscious customers to confusion**
A shopper with a dairy allergy landing on an unfiltered 80-product collection page doesn't browse — they leave. Smart Shop gives every dietary group a clear, immediate path to the products that are safe for them.

**2. Increase average order value with volume discounts**
Set tiered volume discounts (5 items, 10 items, 20 items) directly on your collection page. Smart Shop shows a banner explaining the discount, prompting customers to add more. More items in the basket, higher average order.

**3. Add to cart without leaving the collection**
Smart Shop's morphing quantity stepper lets customers add, increase, and decrease quantities directly on the collection page — no product page clicks, no page reloads. The fastest path from browse to basket in Shopify.

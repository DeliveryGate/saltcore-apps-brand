## App Icons

### Rules
- Canvas: 1200x1200px SVG (export as PNG for submission)
- Corner radius: 24px
- Background: solid app colour (no gradients)
- Foreground: white, single concept
- No text in any icon
- No Shopify logo or branding
- Minimum display size: 64x64px
- Clear space: 10% of icon width on all sides

### Icon Concepts
- SafeServe: Shield with checkmark (safety)
- DeliveryIQ: Calendar with clock overlay (scheduling)
- BlogFlow: Document with sparkle (AI content)
- BundleBox: Gift box with ribbon (bundled value)
- QuoteFlow: Speech bubble with currency symbol (pricing)

## Screenshots

- Dimensions: 1600x900px PNG
- Background: light (#F8FAFC)
- Header bar: app primary colour, 60px height
- App name in header: Inter 600, white
- Content area: actual Polaris UI (real data, not lorem ipsum)
- Footer: "Built by SaltCore" in slate-500, 12px
- Minimum 3 screenshots per app, maximum 5

## Copy Style Guide

### Voice
Professional but approachable. Speak like a knowledgeable colleague, not a sales page.

### Avoid
- "powerful", "seamless", "robust", "cutting-edge", "next-generation"
- Exclamation marks in descriptions
- Feature lists without merchant outcomes

### Use
- Specific, measurable benefits ("Replace 4 apps", "Save £X")
- Lead with merchant outcome, not feature
- Short sentences, active voice
- Numbers over adjectives ("10 bundle types" not "many bundle types")

### Examples
- Good: "Add allergen badges to any product page in 60 seconds"
- Bad: "Powerful allergen management solution for your store"
- Good: "One app replaces four — dates, postcodes, fees, and discounts"
- Bad: "Seamless all-in-one checkout experience"
